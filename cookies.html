Cookies


Description

These homemade cookies are chewy, sweet, and perfect with a glass of milk!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Let the cookies cool before serving. Enjoy!
