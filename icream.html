Ice Cream


Description

This creamy homemade vanilla ice cream is made with just a few ingredients — no ice cream maker needed!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together milk and sugar until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Stir in the heavy cream, vanilla extract, and salt.
  3. If using an ice cream maker, pour mixture in and churn as directed.
  4. If not using an ice cream maker, pour mixture into a freezer-safe container.
  5. Freeze for about 1 hour, then stir vigorously to break up ice crystals.
  6. Repeat stirring every 30–45 minutes for 3–4 hours until creamy.
  7. Scoop and serve once fully frozen. Enjoy!
