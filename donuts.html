Donuts


Description

These homemade donuts are fluffy, golden, and perfect with a dusting of sugar or a chocolate glaze.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add milk, sugar, melted butter, salt, eggs, and 2 cups of flour. Mix well.
  3. Add remaining flour and knead until smooth.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise until doubled (about 1 hour).
  5. Roll out dough and cut into donut shapes.
  6. Let the donuts rise again for 30 minutes.
  7. Heat oil in a deep fryer or pan to 350°F (175°C).
  8. Fry donuts until golden on both sides. Drain on paper towels.
  9. Dust with sugar or glaze as desired. Enjoy!
